Vector Addition Game - Instructions

Welcome to the Vector Addition Game! This game is a fun way to learn and understand the concept of vector addition in 2D space.

How to Play

  1. At the start of each round, two vectors (Vector A and Vector B) will be displayed on the screen.
  2. Your task is to guess the location of the resultant vector (Vector C), which is the sum of Vector A and Vector B.
  3. Click on the canvas where you think the tip of Vector C should be located.
  4. After you make your guess, the actual location of Vector C will be revealed.
  5. The game measures the distance between your guess and the actual vector sum. A shorter distance means a more accurate guess.
  6. The game consists of 5 rounds. Try to be as accurate as possible in each round!

Scoring

Your score is based on the accuracy of your guesses. The cumulative distance over all rounds will determine your final score. The lower the total distance, the better your score!

Have fun and learn about vector addition!
