How to Play

  1. Click on empty cells to place dots.
  2. Try to place as many dots as possible without forming a line of three dots (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  3. The game ends when you can't place any more dots without forming a line of three.
  4. Challenge yourself with different grid sizes!

Remember: No three dots in a row, column, or diagonal!
